The idea

Evidence-driven support, without the overhead of a large firm.

EHR delivers accurate, timely, and methodologically sound research support that strengthens the quality of decision-making. It rests on a strong academic foundation: a PhD in Public Health Sciences from Sweden, an MPH from South Africa, and a BSc in Public Health from Ethiopia — complemented by advanced analytical skill in STATA, SPSS, and R, and professional experience spanning Africa and Sweden.

The business operates primarily online — flexible, low-overhead, and reachable across time zones — so clients get senior-level expertise without senior-firm pricing.

Who it's for

  • Master's & PhD students, in Sweden and abroad
  • University researchers needing statistical support
  • NGOs and civil-society organisations
  • Municipalities & regional health authorities
  • International organisations active in Africa or Sweden

Why EHR

A small practice, built for depth rather than scale.

Deep specialisation

Adolescent mental health and epidemiology expertise, including Group-Based Trajectory Modeling — a method rarely available through small consultancies.

Cross-cultural fluency

20+ years of field-tested experience across Africa and Sweden, from Ebola response in Guinea to national Swedish datasets.

Clarity over jargon

Complex statistical concepts explained in plain, accessible language — for committees, supervisors, and stakeholders alike.

Personal & flexible

A one-person structure means direct access to the consultant on every engagement, with flexible scheduling across time zones.
